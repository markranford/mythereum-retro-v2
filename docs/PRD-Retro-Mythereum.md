# Product Requirements Document: Mythereum Retro V2

## Executive Summary

Mythereum Retro V2 is a strategic card battle game built on the Internet Computer that combines classic retro aesthetics with modern blockchain technology. Players collect heroes, build strongholds, engage in tactical battles, and compete in tournaments while managing their resources and crafting powerful items.

## Vision

Create an immersive, retro-themed card battle experience that rewards strategic thinking, collection building, and competitive play while leveraging the Internet Computer's unique capabilities for true digital ownership and decentralized gameplay.

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Retro Gaming Enthusiasts**: Players who appreciate classic gaming aesthetics and strategic depth
- **Card Game Veterans**: Experienced players from traditional and digital card games seeking new challenges
- **Blockchain Gamers**: Users interested in true digital ownership and decentralized gaming experiences

### Secondary Audience
- **Casual Strategy Players**: Players looking for accessible yet deep strategic gameplay
- **Collectors**: Users who enjoy building and showcasing rare collections
- **Competitive Players**: Tournament-focused players seeking ranked competition

## Core Features

### 1. Player Progression System
Players advance through multiple interconnected progression systems:

- **Experience and Levels**: Gain experience through battles, quests, and achievements
- **Player Accounts**: Persistent profiles tracking stats, history, and accomplishments
- **Skill Development**: Unlock new abilities and strategic options as you progress

### 2. Stronghold Management
Your base of operations and strategic hub:

- **Base Building**: Construct and upgrade your stronghold from level 1 upward
- **Resource Generation**: Unlock passive resource generation through upgrades
- **Defensive Structures**: Build towers and fortifications for strategic advantages
- **Training Facilities**: Enhance hero development and experience gain

### 3. Hero Collection
Build your ultimate deck through hero acquisition and management:

- **Card Collection**: Acquire heroes through battles, market purchases, and crafting
- **Diverse Types**: Multiple hero types with unique abilities and strategic roles
- **Power Levels**: Heroes have varying power levels affecting battle outcomes
- **Deck Building**: Construct strategic decks combining complementary heroes

### 4. Battle System
Engage in strategic card battles:

- **Quick Matches**: Fast-paced battles against other players
- **Practice Mode**: Hone your skills against AI opponents
- **Battle History**: Track your performance and learn from past battles
- **Strategic Depth**: Multiple viable strategies and counter-play options

### 5. Tournament System
Competitive organized play:

- **Scheduled Events**: Regular tournaments with varying formats
- **Rankings and Leaderboards**: Track your competitive standing
- **Prestigious Rewards**: Earn exclusive items and recognition
- **Community Competition**: Face the best players in structured events

### 6. Market Economy
Player-driven trading and commerce:

- **Buy and Sell**: Trade cards and items with other players
- **Price Discovery**: Market-driven pricing for all tradeable items
- **Transaction History**: Transparent record of all market activity
- **Resource Management**: Strategic decisions about buying, selling, and holding

### 7. Crafting System (The Forge)
Create powerful items through crafting:

- **Recipes**: Discover and unlock crafting recipes
- **Material Gathering**: Collect materials through gameplay
- **Item Creation**: Craft cards, equipment, and consumables
- **Enhancement**: Upgrade existing items for improved performance

### 8. Profile and Statistics
Comprehensive player tracking:

- **Personal Stats**: Detailed performance metrics and history
- **Achievement System**: Unlock achievements through gameplay milestones
- **Public Profiles**: Showcase your accomplishments to other players
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your advancement across all systems

### 9. Notifications
Stay informed about game events:

- **Battle Results**: Immediate notification of battle outcomes
- **Tournament Updates**: Alerts for tournament starts, results, and invitations
- **Market Activity**: Notifications for successful trades and new listings
- **System Announcements**: Important game updates and events
